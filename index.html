The
Intentional
Dating Dictionary.

A comprehensive, denotative guide to making stuff happen for the average guy.

Be a man. Read this dictionary. Then get off the computer and BOT.

1. GCB

Gospel-Centered Beauty.

A woman with an inner beauty that makes the soul sing and an outer beauty that is straight up heavenly.

2. BOT

Man up.

When a man is attracted to a woman (see GCB), he approaches her and asks her out, specifically using the word 'date.' This is not easy but is the most crucial step in beginning any intentional relationship.

3. ETF

Establish the feelings.

When a man verbally declares his romantic feelings for a GCB and then demonstrates those feelings by asking her to initial and subsequent DNs.

4. DN

Date night.

When a man takes the time, effort, and money to plan and execute a successful, meaningful, and Jesus-honoring date with a GCB.

5. DTR

Define the relationship.

After a number of DNs, the relationship should be redefined. The man essentially redeclares his feelings and intentions with the GCB and discusses with her the trajectory of the relationship. This includes spiritual, physical, emotional, and social guidelines.

6. ATF

Ask the father.

As prescribed by scripture, the GCB, until the day of marriage, is actually under the authority of a GCD (Gospel-Centered Daddy). The intentional pursuit & marriage of a GCB cannot exclude the acknowledgment, permission, and blessing of the GCB's father.

7. MOK

Man on knee.

When a man declares the ultimate glory of his love for the GCB, and his intentions of sharing his life with her until death.

8. ROF

Ring on finger.

When the GCB graciously accepts the man's offer of marriage.

9. KTB

Kiss the bride.

When a GCD bequeaths the GCB to the man, and in the sight of God, the family, the Church, and biblical leadership, they are wed together in shining glory.

10. 1F

One flesh.

"Therefore a man shall leave his father and his mother and hold fast to his wife, and they shall become one flesh" Genesis 2:24 (ESV).

11. LAO

Live as one.

The man and the GCB live out their days in a manner glorifying to God, multiplying fruitfully just as He intended and discipling their progeny.
